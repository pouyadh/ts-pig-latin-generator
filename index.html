TS Pig Latin Generator

Info

In case you're not quite sure what Pig Latin is, you could read the wikipedia article on Pig Latin

Rules

  • If a word begins with a vowel, just adds "yay" to the end of it. For example, "out" is translated into "outyay".
  • If it begins with a consonant, then we take all consonants before the first vowel and we put them on the end of the word. For example, "which" is translated into "ichwhay".
